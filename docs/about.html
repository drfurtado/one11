Ovande Furtado

I am an associate professor at Cal State Northridge where I teach courses related to Kinesiology. I earned my M.S. and Ph.D.degrees in Motor Behavior from the University of Pittsburgh, PA. Dr.My line of research focuses on two main areas: (1) validation of observational models in psychomotor assessment instruments and (2) the relationship between motor skill competence, perceived motor competence, physical activity levels, and body composition.

If you enjoy the content of this blog, please consider supporting what I do. Thank you.
